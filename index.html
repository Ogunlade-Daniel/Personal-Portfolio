I'm Daniel — a full‑stack web developer based in Nigeria. My path into development started with curiosity about how websites actually work under the hood, and turned into a structured, hands‑on commitment: a 5‑month intensive Fullstack Web Development program at UpTech Computer Training Academy, completed in June 2025.

Over that program I worked across the entire stack — building responsive interfaces with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, then moving into server‑side logic, REST APIs, and relational and document databases. I didn't just learn syntax; I learned how the pieces of a real application fit together.

I'm now applying that foundation to real projects, with a focus on writing clean, readable code and understanding why something works — not just getting it to run. I'm actively looking for opportunities, freelance or full‑time, to keep building.
